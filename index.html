About Us

Mr. Joshua Critchett is an attourney and counsel member of the State Bar of Michigan. He studied law and political science at Wayne State University, where he received his undergraduate degree and juris doctorate. Mr. Critchett is highly qualified and well practiced attorney with over 7 years of experience in and around Detroit, Michigan. Experienced practice areas include, but are not limited to, Criminal (misdemeanor and felony cases), Traffic Tickets, Estate Planning, Wills, Powers of Attorney, Patient Advocate Designations, Trusts, Landlord/ Tenant matters, Divorce, Bankruptcy, and Child Support.
Mr. Critchett is enthusiastic when leading his clients to success weather he is drafting documents or standing in courtroom litigation. Services are available to those located in the county of Wayne, Oakland, Macomb, and the sorrounding areas. If you need counsel, don't wait. Contact us to discuss your legal concerns.
